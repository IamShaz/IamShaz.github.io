My Story

Hey! :) My name is Shazin

I am a multi-talented aspiring entrepreneur who is dedicated to providing quality made websites. My career goals are to advance my skills as a web developer, and further my aspirations of anonline career.

My interest in web development originally stemmed while venturing off into the world of earning money online. Needing my own website, I started browsing and eventually using website themes created by other developers. I found myself limited to what I could customize for my own website, so I began skimming through the lines of code in order to make edits.

I then became fascinated with the different programming languages and how they were being used to form complete websites. Through research and online tutorials, I realized this is something that I wanted to do, and would genuinely enjoy doing it.

I have combined my experience and education in writing and online marketing, to have now dedicated my life to pursuing an online career with a specialization in web development.

In this day and age, knowing how to create a website is a valuable skill to have, it’s equivalent to knowing how to build a house. A website is someone’s property, land, their real-estate on the online world.

I am quite knowledgeable in creating websites that can attract the visitors needed through my web development skills, written content and various online marketing strategies.

A website can be incredibly valuable, but only if you know how to leverage it.
